02. SANJAYA


SANJAYA is an intelligent path guiding robot helmet for assisting
virtually impaired person. Equipped with digital camera and advance
microprocessor (RaspberryPI) for sensing the type of object and
calculating distance from it, then informing the blind using vibration
motors and voice instructions.
